Our Story
The
Legacy

Founded in 2008, Chennai Super Kings has grown into one of the most successful and beloved franchises in cricket history. Led for most of its journey by the legendary MS Dhoni, CSK became more than just a team—it became an emotion that unites millions of fans across the globe. With five IPL championships, two Champions League T20 titles, and a record of consistent excellence, the Men in Yellow have set the benchmark for success, resilience, and loyalty. Through triumphs, comebacks, and unforgettable moments, CSK has built a legacy that transcends generations.

"Whistle Podu" is not just a slogan—it's the heartbeat of a global family that proudly wears yellow and celebrates the spirit of Chennai Super Kings.
